Types of Coffee

Affogato Coffee

Affogato is a coffee-based dessert consisting of vanilla gelato or ice cream topped with hot espresso. It's not typically considered a beverage but is instead enjoyed slowly or with a spoon.

Americano Coffee

The Americano is a classic coffee drink made by diluting espresso with hot water, resulting in a bold yet smooth flavor profile.

Black Coffee

Black coffee is as simple as it gets with ground coffee beans steeped in hot water, served warm. And if you want to sound fancy, you can call black coffee by its proper name: cafe noir.

Flat White Coffee

This Aussie-born drink is basically a cappuccino without the foam or chocolate sprinkle. It’s an espresso drink with steamed milk.

Frappe Coffee

A frappe coffee is a chilled coffee drink made by blending coffee with ice, milk, sugar, and flavorings. It's known for its frothy texture and is often topped with whipped cream.

Ice Coffee

A chilled coffee beverage made by pouring brewed coffee over ice. It's often served with milk, cream, or sweeteners, and can be customized with flavorings or toppings like whipped cream.

Irish Coffee

Irish coffee consists of black coffee, whiskey and sugar, topped with whipped cream.

Latte Coffee

It is comprised of a shot of espresso and steamed milk with just a touch of foam. It can be ordered plain or with a flavor shot of anything from vanilla to pumpkin spice.

Macchiato Coffee

The macchiato is another espresso-based drink that has a small amount of foam on top. It’s the happy medium between a cappuccino and a doppio.

Mocha Coffee

The mocha is a chocolate espresso drink with steamed milk and foam.

Ristretto Coffee

A ristretto is a short shot of espresso coffee made with the same amount of coffee but half the amount of water, resulting in a more concentrated and intense flavor.

Turkish Coffee

Finely ground coffee simmered with water (and sugar, optionally) in a cezve or ibrik, producing a strong, rich brew with a thick, foamy texture.

Redeye Coffee

Redeye coffee is a beverage made by adding a shot of espresso to a cup of drip-brewed coffee, resulting in a stronger and more caffeinated drink.

Nitro Coffee

Nitro coffee is cold brew coffee infused with nitrogen gas, typically served on tap like a draft beer. This process gives the coffee a smooth, creamy texture and a frothy head, enhancing its flavor and mouthfeel.

Espresso Coffee

A chilled coffee beverage made by pouring brewed coffee over ice. It's often served with milk, cream, or sweeteners, and can be customized with flavorings or toppings like whipped cream.

Doppio Coffee

Doppio coffee is an espresso beverage made with a double shot of espresso, resulting in a stronger and more intense flavor compared to a single shot.

Cortado Coffee

A cortado is a coffee drink made with equal parts espresso and steamed milk, creating a balanced and creamy beverage with a strong coffee flavor.

Cold Brew Coffee

Brewed with cold water over 12-24 hours, it has a smooth, mellow flavor with less acidity than hot brewed coffee.

Café au Lait Coffee

Café au lait is a coffee drink made with equal parts of brewed coffee and steamed milk, creating a creamy and balanced beverage.

Cappuccino Coffee

Cappuccino coffee is an espresso-based drink consisting of equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, resulting in a creamy and frothy beverage.

Drip Coffee

Drip coffee is brewed by pouring hot water over ground coffee beans contained in a filter, allowing the water to drip through and extract flavor, resulting in a smooth and flavorful beverage.
